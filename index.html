About Me

During my thirty year career in telecom I worked with code developers but never coded myself. Along comes 2020 and it is time to learn some new skills. I have a passion for technology and travel (cruising is one of my most favorite ways to travel) and plan to use my new skills to bring the two together. As a travel advisor I like to make travel affordable and easy for my clients. I enjoy spending time with family and friends and am looking forward to the time that we can all gather safely once again.

Using technology to simplify travel, to save time with routine tasks and give my clients more time for fun and new experiences on their trips is my goal!
